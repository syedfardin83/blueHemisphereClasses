About


How will the course run?

These courses are completely live class and customized based on every person. There will be a specific expected time for every course to complete.

Two Days' free trial.

If you want to test the teachers or talk to them, there is a free trial for 2 days.
The first day is for sharing my experience, your experience, your demands and your expectations.
On the second day, we will teach on the basis of your demands. The further is your choice.

Pricings and discount.

There will be a specific price set for each course which you will find on courses page here. You will have to pay that amount after every session, i.e. you will have to pay daily. In case the course is unable to complete in the specified time, you will get 10% discount on the price, every day.

What's the purpose of this?

We are a team whose aim is to spread knowledge of coding as much as possible, and make programmers in schools and collages comfortable with not only writing programs, but also with theory.
